What is snailposting?

Snailposting is a legendary 4chan /biz/ tradition where an anonymous poster commits to posting a snail emoji every single day until Bitcoin reaches $1,000,000.

The Origin

It started as a simple daily ritual: "Day X of snailposting every day until BTC is $1M." What began as one anon's dedication became a cultural phenomenon.

Why the Snail?

The snail is slow but steady. It doesn't rush. It doesn't panic sell. It just keeps moving forward. The snail is the ultimate diamond hands.
