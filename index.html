The Snailposting Tradition

What is snailposting?

Snailposting is a legendary 4chan /biz/ tradition where an anonymous poster commits to posting a snail emoji every single day until Bitcoin reaches $1,000,000.

The Origin

It started as a simple daily ritual: "Day X of snailposting every day until BTC is $1M." What began as one anon's dedication became a cultural phenomenon, with the snail representing patience, persistence, and unwavering belief in the long game.

Why the Snail?

The snail is slow but steady. It doesn't rush. It doesn't panic sell. It just keeps moving forward, day after day, no matter what the market does. The snail is the ultimate diamond hands.

Our Mission

We've taken this legendary tradition and created our own version. Instead of waiting for BTC to hit $1M, we're snailposting until OUR token hits $10M marketcap. Every holder is part of the journey. Every day, the snail grows.
